Make Planets (MEAN stack web-application)

Make Planets is a website that gives science fiction authors a comprehensive toolbelt to design their own unique and realisitc planetary systems. Users can check the stability of their systems and edit it on the fly, see the sizes of planets and moons in each others skies, quickly design orbital resonances between sibling planets and moons, and so much more. Afterwards, the custom star systems can be exported to 3rd-party programs such as SpaceEngine to view it in immersive 3D.

Make Planets was built using the MEAN stack, running on Angular 6.

LocatoBot (BaseHacks Hackathon - Top Ten Winner, Best Location-Based Hack, Best Conversation/Interaction Bot)

LocatoBot averages the location of all participants in a Telegram group chat, and then finds restaurants, etc. in that general location. It is intended to help faciltate meetups.

LocatoBot was programmed in JavaScript, using the Google Places API as well as the GupShup bot platform.
